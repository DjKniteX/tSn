The Boxer Classics

The Boxer Classics is a series of tournaments for both Starcraft Remastered and Warcraft 3 Reforged as a community-cup style to relive one of the greatest RTSs as well as the pioneers of Esports. The community prize pool is serviced by donations and both run as a two day event with the first to happen in the Winter of 2019. Open to all, open to any skill level, open to anyone willing to have fun.
