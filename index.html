About Us

"More GG. More Skill" - Aleksey "White-Ra" Krupnyk.

Started out gaming together with family in 2001 when all the cousins and relatives got their computers online either by dial-up or ethernet, it was a time to be alive. Playing Diablo 2 first was the game that started our online obssession.

Introduced to Runescape early on and then Ragnarok Online shortly after, we decided to start a forum (that didn't live long) and recruit members to various games with us. Moving on to games such as Counter-Strike, Day of Defeat and MapleStory, we played everything the team can get their hands on. 2011 is when we introduced the Esports division. The team wasn't very keen on the idea but we still sponsored a small number of players to play in various tournaments online.

2015, we set up our Discord and some of our members started streaming. Aside from gaming, we also sponsored community events, got artists to join the team to promote positivity, and now we gotten into game development. 2020 is still very much alive and we're just as active as ever.

  • Video games is our passion and willing to work with anyone wanting to compete at a competitive/professional level.
  • We are releasing our own homemade projects such as indie games and mixtapes.
  • We also partner with other organizations to sponsor various community events in the area or online.

What We Do

We started out as a gaming clan but we branched out to other things as well.

VG Community

An ever growing VG community still playing and competing in games since 2003. Catch us online in many different games as well as "the place we call home" in Runescape, Ragnarok Online and Phantasy Star Online. Let's play some games.

Creative Circle

Aside from Playing games, A few members of Team SKYNITE has created music and performed live in front of fans at various events across the country. One day, you'll hear one of us on the radio. Check out a few: Shoko and Wondae. As well as tune in for our upcoming Podcast Series!

ESPORTS

Possibily the first if not one of the first few Esports team to emerge in Arkansas with the release of Starcraft 2. As we get older and unable to compete as much, we still sponsor players and tournaments with cash prizes as well as preparing for our future amateur leagues.
