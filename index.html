TeamSky Nite

Originally founded as Superior Chaos Pirates in 2002 a Brood War and Diablo clan that never took off. It wasn't until the spring of 2004 where they picked up PSO and modeled themselves after German PSO Team, Mia-Team. The clan was originally just a group of kids who wanted to play games together in many different genres which they continued to do so today. In 2011, Team Sky Nite was originally formed from the remnants of Superior Chaos Pirates and decided to take on more of a local organization role. They have sponsored local events as well as hosted the tSn brood War Cup in 2015. With no actual titles or wins for the organization, they decided to branch off and start the Esports division in 2019. From playing together as a team to now forming an Esports divsion, Team Sky Nite Esports Club is looking to move forward with the new team and compete when they can.
